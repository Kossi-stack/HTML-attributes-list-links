My Favorite Travel Destinations

Here are some of my favorite places to visit:

  • Paris, France

    Paris is one of the most romantic cities in the world and it has something for everyone. From the world-famous Eiffel Tower to the iconic Louvre museum, there is something for everyone in this beautiful city.

    • Must-See Attractions: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre Dame Cathedral
    • Must-Do Activities: Take a boat tour along the Seine, enjoy a picnic in the Luxembourg Gardens, go shopping in the Champs-Elysees

  • Rome, Italy

    Rome is a city steeped in history and culture. From the Colosseum to the Vatican, there is much to explore in this city. The food is also amazing and there are many restaurants and cafes to explore.

    • Must-See Attractions: Colosseum, Pantheon, St. Peter's Basilica
    • Must-Do Activities: Take a food tour of the city, explore the ruins of the Roman Forum, visit the Trevi Fountain

  • New York City, USA

    New York City has something for everyone. From the bright lights of Times Square to the iconic Statue of Liberty, this city has it all. There are many great restaurants, bars, and shops to explore as well.

    • Must-See Attractions: Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, Central Park
    • Must-Do Activities: Take a boat tour around Manhattan, explore the many museums, go shopping in Soho
